About Diseases

Diseases are abnormal conditions that affect the body or mind. They can be caused by infections, lifestyle, genetics, or environmental factors. Early detection is important to prevent serious complications.

Consequences of Diseases

Diseases can lead to physical discomfort, emotional distress, and financial burden. They may reduce quality of life and can be life-threatening if not treated properly. Prevention and timely medical care are crucial to minimize these consequences.
